Christopher is a researcher in the CleverHans Lab at the Vector Institute and an incoming AI Resident 2020 at Google. His research interests are in machine learning and its intersections with security and privacy (e.g., adversarial ML, deep learning, differential privacy, etc.). He has worked at Intel Corp. and Georgian Partners LP as a machine learning researcher and research engineer, respectively, where he worked on AutoML, a branch of research focusing on automatically creating machine learning models, and deep learning for bug triaging. Currently, he is focusing on data privacy in deep learning.
